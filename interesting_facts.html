Ferret Classification

Kingdom Animalia
Phylum Chordata
Class Mammalia
Order Carnivora
Suborder Caniformia
Family Mustelidae
Genus Mustela
Species M. putorius

  • The name "ferret" is derived from the Latin furittus, meaning "little thief"
  • Ferrets spend 14–18 hours a day asleep
  • A group of ferrets is commonly referred to as a "business"
  • If excited, ferrets may perform a behaviour called the "weasel war dance"
  • Ferrets have 4 types of teeth: incisors, canines, premolar teeth, and molars
  • Ferrets are an important experimental animal model for human influenza
